Restore This Place

You are tasked with restoring a room to bring out the true vintage character where you will be choosing your own assets. What you don't know is how many of each item you will need. You will need to choose wisely.

Each item contains a "secret value" - mix and match the items to eventually equal the Target value for each restoration project. Be sure not to go over the Target Value, otherwise you lose. Good luck!
